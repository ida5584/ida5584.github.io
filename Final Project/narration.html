Narrator Script

Repeat this cycle until the game has ended. (Check outline for gameover conditions)


NightTime

It is night time. Everyone go to sleep.

If you wake up in the middle of the night to complete your task make sure to stay quiet and refer to each player by their assigned numbers.

Werewolves

Werewolves wake up and choose who you want to kill. All werewolves must agree on one person.

This is the person you want to kill? Okay. Go back to sleep.

Mage

Mage wake up. This person was killed. You have a antidote, would you like to use this antidote?

You have a poison, would you like to use this poison? Who do you want to poison? Okay. Go back to sleep.

Seer

Seer wake up. Who would you like to investigate? This person is (give a hand signal for good or bad).

~ Repeat This person is (give a hand signal for good or bad). Okay. Go back to sleep.

Hunter

Only on the first night!

Hunter wake up. Okay. Go back to sleep.

DayTime

Daytime! Everyone wake up. This person died. (Player who died) cannot speak because (he/she/they) died in the night. You may not speak for the remainder of the game.

Starting from player numerically after (Player who died) and going clockwise, take your turn to speak.

Voting!

Now that everyone has had a turn to speak. Vote for one player and the player who gets the majority of the votes will be exiled.

Three, two, one!

(Player) has the most votes. (To that player) You may now have one last speech and you will be exiled.
