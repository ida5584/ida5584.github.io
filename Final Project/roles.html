Specials

Mage

You reside on the good side, in the Special’s faction. You wake up right after the Wolves. You have one antidote to save one player and one poison to kill one player. The narrator will tell you which player has died so long as you still have the antidote.

Once you have used the antidote you will no longer have that information. You may only use your skills when you are awake at night and if you are alive and you may only use one skill per night.

Seer

You reside on the good side, in the Special’s faction. You wake up after both the Wolves and the Mage. You may investigate one player during the night and will receive information on whether the player is good or bad but not what faction the player is in.

You may choose to share this information with the group during discussion. You may only use your skills when you are awake at night and if you are alive.

Hunter

You reside on the good side, in the Special’s faction. You only wake up the first night so the narrator can confirm your role. You do nothing during the night. When your death is announced, you may activate your skill and choose a player to shoot.

   If you die by poison from the Mage you may not use your skill.      


Wolves

Wolf

You reside on the bad side, in the Wolverine’s faction. You and your wolf partners must agree on one player to kill during the night.

There are no restrictions for who you want to kill, meaning you may choose to kill one of your own or even to kill no one.

Civilians

Civilian

You reside on the good side, in the Civilian’s faction. You are a sitting duck but if you are good at this game you may protect the Specials by lying during the discussion.

Even though you have no skills you still have a vote and can contribute to the death during the day. Listen intently during the discussion to try to figure out everyone’s roles.
