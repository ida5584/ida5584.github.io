Start Of The Game

The game begins with a total of 10 people, 9 players and 1 narrator. The 9 players sit in a circle and each player is assigned a unique number, starting from 1 until all 9 players obtain a number. The narrator now deals cards (ie. roles) and players see their roles in numerical order. Once everyone is familiar with their roles (role descriptions can be found by clicking on the Roles tab) the narrator begins the narration (script can be found by clicking on the Narration tab.)

Factions

There must be an emphasis on the three factions even if the Specials and Civilians are fundamentally working together because the Werewolf faction can win by just eliminating every player of one faction. See below for more details and examples of the requirements for the game to end.

End Of The Game

Werewolves win if all the Civilians die or all the Specials die. Some (not all) examples include:

  • 1 Werewolf, Mage, Seer, Hunter
  • 1 Werewolf, 3 Civilians
  • 2 Werewolves, Mage, Seer, Hunter
  • 3 Werewolves, 2 Civilians

Civilians and Specials win if all the Werewolves die before one of their factions wipe out.

  • Mage, Seer, Hunter, 1 Civilian
  • Mage, Seer, 2 Civilian
  • Seer, 3 Civilian
  • Hunter, 1 Civilian
